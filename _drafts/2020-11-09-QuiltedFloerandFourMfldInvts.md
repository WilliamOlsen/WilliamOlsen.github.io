title: Quilted Floer cohomology and invariants of four-manifolds
author: William E. Olsen
tags: Quilted Floer cohomology, surface fibrations, wrinkled fibrations, Lagrangian matching invariants
---

We demonstrate how Tim Perutz's Lagrangian matching invariants for near-symplectic four-manifolds equipped with compatible broken fibrations fit into the framework of cyclic cohomology and give some evidence for how these invariants compare to the mixed invariants of Ozsvath and Szabo.
