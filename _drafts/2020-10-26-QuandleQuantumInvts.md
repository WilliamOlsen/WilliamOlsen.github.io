title: Trisections, quandle cocycles, and quantum invariants
author: William E. Olsen
tags: trisections, quandles, quantum invariants
---

We review a construction of Jason Joseph who shows how to recover the quandle 3-cocyle invariants from a surface in trisected bridge position. Using this construction as motivation, we demonstrate how to export Joseph's techniques into the setting of quantum topology and its invariants.
